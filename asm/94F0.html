94F0: Back To BASIC
BackToBasic 94F0 LD B,$32 Set a counter in B of 32.
BackToBasic_Loop 94F2 HALT Halt operation (suspend CPU until the next interrupt).
94F3 DJNZ BackToBasic_Loop Decrease halt counter by one and loop back to BackToBasic_Loop until the counter is zero.
94F5 JP $0000 Jump to START.
